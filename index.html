Commands

/text [prompt]
Open a persistent AI chat thread — keep typing to continue the conversation.
/image [prompt]
Generate an image from a text prompt using Flux or any connected image model.
/audio [text]
Convert text to speech using 11 OpenAI TTS voices including Nova, Echo, and Onyx.
/video [prompt]
Generate a video clip. Requires Pollen credits via your connected account.
/model [type] [name]
Switch the AI model with autocomplete — text, image, audio, or video.
/connect
Link your Pollinations account via device flow to unlock paid models and credits.
/remember [key] [value]
Save info NovAI will remember about you across sessions.
/models · /info · /help
List available models, view your current settings, or see all commands.
